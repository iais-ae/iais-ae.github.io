About Us

IGG ASELSAN Integrated Systems LLC (IAIS) is a joint venture company established in 2011 between International Golden Group and ASELSAN. IAIS specialises in the development and manufacture of Remote Controlled Weapon Systems (RCWS) with a wide range of applications.

The first of its kind in the UAE, its focus lies with on-the-ground installations and full integration and assembly of the systems, as well as the technical and logistical support required.

With ASELSAN focusing on the technical and logistical support and IGG providing marketing and sales support, IAIS is well placed to cater to the needs of the UAE and the GCC region with offices and a production facility in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.
